Name of your enterprise: STUDENT SQUARE

Summary of your enterprise:

Student Square is a platform for educational counseling and career development for students. We support and advise students in making informed decisions about their studies, such as educational plans, career goals, and college or university selection based on their abilities and preferences. We help students cope with personal concerns that are interfering with their academic progress, and we also work with the student's parents or guardians to help them figure out the best solutions to the student's problems. We also give the appropriate mentoring at every stage of their academic and professional careers.

How is your enterprise reducing poverty?

Poverty is defined as a state or condition in which an individual or a group lacks the financial means and necessities for a basic level of living. Bangladesh is still a developing country. Poverty remains a big concern, notwithstanding rapid economic progress. We're working to address this social issue by building a strong, capable, engaged, and competent community that can contribute to a person's ability to conduct employment that generates economic value. We believe that education is the most effective way to enhance human capital while simultaneously contributing directly to economic prosperity.

Our methodology

Seminars, group sessions, one-on-one counseling, and family counseling are all used to help students develop goals. We consider the types of talent, talent's inclination, the student's and his or her family's expectations, and other factors. Then we split them up into four or five groups and prepare them for the admissions battle. We provide ongoing mental support and inspiration during that period because mental stress is a game changer in the admissions era. We also provide financial assistance to needy students to the extent that we are able. In our organization, there is a medical student team, an engineering student team, and scientific and arts background students' teams.

Problem

One of the most crucial of our fundamental human rights is education. However, our educational system is wholly results-driven, rather than inventive. In our society, an increasing number of students are approaching the point where their academic lives are meaningless. There are some concerns with the following topics:

  1. A lack of interest in studying
  2. Uncertainty about your career
  3. The Academic and Ethical Conundrum
  4. A lack of proper information
  5. A lack of concentration
  6. A lack of awareness
  7. Diminishes the educational value
  8. A lack of materials
  9. problems of poverty and drop out (especially female students)

Solution

We must solve the existing issues with the materials that we already have in our country. We want to introduce a new and effective system for improving the basic skills among the students range from class 9 to undergrad students through one-to-one counselling. The counselors will be the immediate seniors of the students who have already faced these problems from different sectors. And then counselled students will be trained to become professional counselors. Thus, we are creating a chain of connection among students for sustainability.
